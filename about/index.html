About Clayflow

We Believe Business Software
Should Be Beautiful

Clayflow was founded in 2022 by three engineers who were tired of ugly, bloated business tools. We set out to build a platform that feels as good as it works — where design and function are one.

Our Story

From Frustration to Innovation

Every member of our founding team had spent years wrestling with clunky enterprise software that looked like it was designed in 2005. Dashboards that required a manual to navigate. Workflows that took more clicks than they saved. Notifications that never quite told you what you needed to know.

So we built Clayflow — a workspace that combines the power of enterprise-grade tools with the polish and simplicity of modern consumer apps. No learning curve, no bloat, just a calm, capable command center for your business.

Our Mission & Values

What Drives Us

Every decision we make is guided by these core principles.

Craft Over Cargo

We obsess over every pixel, every micro-interaction, every word. Software should feel delightful, not dutiful.

People First

Our customers, our team, our community — we build for humans. Empathy is our north star.

Radical Simplicity

Every feature must earn its place. If it doesn't make life simpler, it doesn't ship.

Trust by Default

Security isn't an afterthought. We earn your trust with transparency, privacy, and enterprise-grade protection.

Global by Design

Remote-first, culturally inclusive, and built for teams that span time zones and continents.

Continuous Evolution

We ship weekly, learn constantly, and never settle. The best version of Clayflow is always ahead.

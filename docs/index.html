Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Your free trial includes full access to all features for 14 days with no credit card required. After the trial, choose a plan that fits your needs or export your data at any time.

Can I integrate with existing tools?

Yes! Clayflow offers native integrations with over 200 popular tools including Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, and more. Our REST API and webhooks allow custom integrations with any platform.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Our infrastructure is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. We also offer dedicated private cloud deployments for enterprise customers.

What kind of support do you offer?

All plans include email support with a 4-hour response time. Pro and Enterprise plans include priority live chat and phone support. Enterprise customers also get a dedicated account manager.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your data remains accessible in read-only mode for 30 days, giving you plenty of time to export everything you need.

FAQ

Got Questions?

We've got answers. If not, reach out anytime.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

You get full access to all Pro features for 14 days. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose a plan or continue with our free Starter tier.

Can I switch plans at any time?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you'll be prorated. When you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Enterprise plans also get SSO, SAML, and on-premise deployment options.

What integrations do you support?

Over 200 integrations including Slack, GitHub, Jira, Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and more. We also offer a REST API and webhooks for custom connections.
